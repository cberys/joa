Where do I download Java OceanAtlas?

  • The newest version of Java OceanAtlas should be available for download from http://joa.ucsd.edu/

How do I update my version of Java OceanAtlas?

  • Updates are occasionally available at the JOA website, http://joa.ucsd.edu/.
using dpo-examples with the dpo textbook

How do I find the DPO-examples that correspond with a specific textbook chapter?

  • Click on CHAPTERS (left-hand navigation bar) and then choose from the exercises associated with a specific textbook chapter, or
  • Click on SITE MAP (left-hand navigation bar) and then choose from the exercises associated with a specific textbook chapter
